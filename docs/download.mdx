## Installation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the .dmg file">
    Click the download button above or visit the [releases page](https://github.com/Benedict-Carling/eyeryhthm/releases/latest) to get the latest version
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the disk image">
    Double-click the downloaded `.dmg` file to mount it
  </Step>
  <Step title="Drag to Applications">
    Drag the EyeRhythm app to your Applications folder
  </Step>
  <Step title="First launch (important)">
    **Right-click** the app and select **"Open"** to bypass Gatekeeper on first launch. Click "Open" in the dialog that appears.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grant camera permission">
    When prompted, click "Allow" to grant camera access. EyeRhythm needs this to detect your blinks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run calibration">
    Follow the on-screen calibration wizard to personalize blink detection to your eyes
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="'EyeRhythm' cannot be opened because the developer cannot be verified">
    This is normal for apps downloaded outside the Mac App Store. To fix:

    1. **Right-click** (or Control-click) the app in Finder
    2. Select **"Open"** from the context menu
    3. Click **"Open"** in the dialog

    Alternatively:
    1. Go to **System Settings > Privacy & Security**
    2. Scroll down to find the message about EyeRhythm
    3. Click **"Open Anyway"**
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Camera not detected or 'No camera access'">
    EyeRhythm needs camera permission to function:

    1. Open **System Settings > Privacy & Security > Camera**
    2. Find EyeRhythm in the list
    3. Toggle the switch to **ON**
    4. Restart EyeRhythm

    If EyeRhythm isn't in the list, try launching it again - macOS should prompt you for permission.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="App crashes on launch">
    Try resetting the app's data:

    1. Quit EyeRhythm completely (check Activity Monitor if needed)
    2. Open Finder and press **Cmd+Shift+G**
    3. Enter: `~/Library/Application Support/`
    4. Delete the `EyeRhythm` folder
    5. Relaunch the app

    Still having issues? [Open an issue on GitHub](https://github.com/Benedict-Carling/eyeryhthm/issues/new)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Face not detected">
    For best results:

    - Ensure good lighting on your face (avoid backlighting)
    - Position yourself at arm's length from the camera
    - Make sure your full face is visible in the preview
    - Remove glasses with heavy reflections if possible
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Auto-update not working">
    EyeRhythm checks for updates automatically. If updates aren't installing:

    1. Check if you have the latest version on the [releases page](https://github.com/Benedict-Carling/eyeryhthm/releases)
    2. Download and install the latest `.dmg` manually
    3. The app will auto-update from then on
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
